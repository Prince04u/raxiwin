Privacy Policy

How Raxiwin collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information — and the rights you have over your data.

Overview

Your privacy is one of our highest priorities at Raxiwin. This Privacy Policy explains in plain language what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and what rights you have over it. We have written this policy to be as readable as possible while still meeting our legal obligations under applicable data protection laws.

By using Raxiwin you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any aspect of how we handle personal data, please discontinue use of the platform and contact our Data Protection Officer to request deletion of any data we already hold.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information in several ways: information you provide directly when registering, depositing, contacting support, or participating in promotions; information generated automatically as you use the platform; and information received from third parties such as identity verification providers and payment processors.

Information You Provide

This includes your name, date of birth, email address, mobile number, residential address, government identification documents, payment instrument details, and any messages or documents you send to our support team.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use Raxiwin we automatically collect technical information including your IP address, device type, operating system, browser version, language preferences, referring URL, and a record of your activity on the platform — such as games played, bets placed, deposits made, and pages visited.

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from KYC providers, fraud-prevention services, payment processors, and analytics partners. This information is used to verify your identity, prevent fraud, and improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information for the following purposes: to provide and operate the Raxiwin platform; to verify your identity and prevent fraud, money laundering, and underage gambling; to process deposits, withdrawals, and bets; to communicate with you about your account, promotions, and platform updates; to comply with legal and regulatory obligations; to detect, investigate, and resolve security incidents; and to improve our products through analytics and research.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with marketers outside of our affiliate programme. We do not use your data for advertising purposes outside of our own platform.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Raxiwin uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve the platform. Essential cookies are required for authentication, security, and core functionality and cannot be disabled. Analytics cookies help us understand how players use the site so we can improve it. Marketing cookies, where used, are subject to your explicit consent.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or through the cookie banner displayed on first visit. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share personal information only with parties who have a legitimate need to access it. These include: our payment processors and banking partners; KYC and fraud-prevention vendors; cloud hosting and security providers; legal and regulatory authorities, where required by law; and, in the event of a merger or acquisition, the acquiring entity.

Every third party with access to your data is contractually obligated to handle it in accordance with this policy and applicable data protection law. We conduct due diligence on all vendors before engaging them and review their security practices on an ongoing basis.

International Data Transfers

Raxiwin operates globally and your personal information may be processed in countries other than your country of residence. Where data is transferred outside your jurisdiction, we put in place appropriate safeguards — including standard contractual clauses approved by relevant data protection authorities — to ensure that your data continues to receive a high level of protection.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter, in accordance with our legal obligations. Specifically: identity verification documents are retained for at least five years following account closure, in line with anti-money-laundering rules; transaction records are retained for at least seven years, in line with financial record-keeping requirements; gameplay logs are retained for a minimum of two years for dispute resolution and audit purposes.

Where data is no longer required for any legitimate purpose, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data: the right to access the data we hold about you; the right to correct inaccurate data; the right to request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations; the right to restrict or object to certain processing; the right to data portability; and the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at the email address provided below. We will respond within the time frame required by applicable law, typically 30 days.

Security

We take the security of your personal information very seriously. Technical safeguards include encryption in transit and at rest, regular vulnerability scanning, penetration testing by independent security firms, intrusion detection systems, and strict access controls based on the principle of least privilege.

Organizational safeguards include mandatory security training for all employees, background checks for personnel with access to sensitive data, separation of duties for high-risk operations, and a documented incident response plan that is exercised regularly.

Despite these measures, no system can be guaranteed completely secure. If a security incident affects your data we will notify you and the relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Raxiwin is intended only for adults of legal gambling age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will delete it immediately and close any associated account.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. Material changes will be notified to registered users by email and through a prominent notice on the platform.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@raxiwin.com. We are committed to responding to all privacy-related inquiries promptly and thoroughly.

Legal Bases for Processing

Where data protection law requires us to identify a legal basis for processing your personal data, we rely on the following: performance of our contract with you, in order to operate your account and process your transactions; compliance with our legal obligations, particularly in respect of anti-money-laundering and responsible gaming; legitimate interests, including fraud prevention, security, product improvement, and direct marketing to existing customers; and your explicit consent, where required for specific processing activities such as marketing to prospective customers or the use of non-essential cookies.

Where processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal.

Automated Decision-Making

Certain decisions on the Raxiwin platform are made or supported by automated systems. These include real-time fraud-prevention checks that may decline a deposit or withdrawal, responsible-gaming triggers that may prompt outreach from our team, and bonus-eligibility checks. In every case where an automated decision could have a significant effect on you, you have the right to request human review of the decision and to provide additional context that may change the outcome.

Marketing Preferences

We may send you marketing communications about new games, promotions, tournaments, and product updates, where you have not opted out. You can manage your marketing preferences at any time through your account settings, and every marketing email contains a clear one-click unsubscribe link. Unsubscribing from marketing does not affect service-related communications such as transaction confirmations and security notices.

Profiling for Player Protection

We analyze patterns of play and account activity to identify behavior that may indicate harm. This profiling is carried out for the legitimate interest of protecting players and is subject to oversight by our responsible-gaming team. You have the right to object to this processing, although doing so may reduce our ability to support you proactively.

Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting personal data, we will assess the incident in line with our documented response plan. Where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of affected individuals, we will notify those individuals and the relevant supervisory authority within the timelines required by applicable law. Notifications will include a clear description of the incident, the personal data affected, the steps we are taking to address it, and the steps individuals should consider taking themselves.

Subject Access Requests in Detail

When you submit a request to access the personal data we hold about you, we will verify your identity to prevent disclosure to an unauthorized party. Verification typically involves logging in to your account and answering security questions, and may require submission of a government-issued ID for closed or compromised accounts.

Once verified, we will provide a structured export of your personal data within 30 days, extendable by a further 60 days for complex requests. The export includes your profile data, transaction history, communications with support, and a record of any automated decisions affecting you. Some data — such as information about third parties or internal risk assessments — may be redacted in accordance with applicable law.

Data Protection Officer

Raxiwin has appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) with primary responsibility for monitoring our compliance with applicable data protection law, providing advice on data protection impact assessments, and acting as a point of contact for both data subjects and supervisory authorities. The DPO can be reached at dpo@raxiwin.com.

Children Below the Legal Gambling Age

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below the legal gambling age in their jurisdiction. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided personal data to Raxiwin, they should contact us immediately so that the data can be deleted and any account closed. We support and encourage the use of parental-control software, and we provide guidance for parents on our Responsible Gaming page.

Updates and Version History

We maintain a version history of this Privacy Policy and will provide the previous version on request. Each substantive update is communicated to registered users by email and noted in a 'last updated' line at the top of this page when next published.

Categories of Personal Data in Detail

To help you understand exactly what we hold, here is a more detailed breakdown of the categories of personal data we may process. Identification data includes your full name, date of birth, gender (where provided), nationality, and government identification numbers. Contact data includes email address, mobile number, and residential address. Account data includes your username, password (stored in hashed form), security questions, and account preferences. Financial data includes payment method details, transaction history, wagering activity, and tax-related information where applicable. Verification data includes copies of identification documents, proof of address, and proof of payment instrument ownership. Behavioral data includes records of games played, time spent on the platform, marketing interactions, and use of responsible-gaming tools. Technical data includes IP addresses, device identifiers, browser type and version, operating system, and session logs.

Specific Purposes of Processing

Each category of data is processed for one or more specific, identified purposes. Identification and contact data are used to establish and maintain your account and to communicate with you. Financial data is used to process deposits, withdrawals, and bets, and to comply with anti-money-laundering and tax obligations. Verification data is used to confirm your identity and to prevent fraud. Behavioral data is used to improve our products, to detect risky patterns of play, and — with your consent — to personalize promotional offers. Technical data is used to deliver the service, to maintain security, and to investigate incidents.

Storage Locations

Personal data is stored in secure data centers located in jurisdictions that provide an adequate level of data protection as recognized by the European Commission or, where appropriate, under standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards. Our primary storage regions are the European Union and India, with secondary storage in selected other jurisdictions for resilience and disaster recovery purposes. Specific storage location information can be provided on request.

Vendor Management

We maintain a detailed register of every third-party vendor that processes personal data on our behalf. Each vendor is subject to a written data processing agreement that imposes contractual obligations consistent with this Privacy Policy and with applicable law. Vendors are reviewed at onboarding and on an annual basis thereafter, including assessment of their security posture, data protection practices, and financial stability. We will only continue to work with vendors that meet our standards.

Profiling and Player Protection

Our responsible-gaming systems use behavioral analytics to identify patterns that may indicate harm. These patterns include but are not limited to rapid increases in deposit frequency or amount, unusually long session durations, chasing-loss behavior, repeated reversal of withdrawal requests, and significant deviation from a player's historical play patterns. Where such patterns are detected, our team may apply soft interventions such as in-product messaging or proactive outreach, or, in more serious cases, mandatory cooling-off periods or account restrictions. These measures are taken in the legitimate interest of preventing harm to players and to the integrity of the platform.

Right to Object

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data where that processing is based on our legitimate interests, including direct marketing and certain forms of profiling. Where you object to direct marketing, we will stop processing your data for that purpose immediately. Where you object to other processing based on legitimate interest, we will assess whether our interests override your objection and will respond to you in writing within the time frame required by applicable law.

Right Not to Be Subject to Solely Automated Decisions

You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing — including profiling — that produces legal effects on you or significantly affects you in a similar way. Where any such decision is made, you have the right to obtain human intervention, to express your point of view, and to contest the decision.

Cross-Border Transfers in Detail

Where personal data is transferred from one jurisdiction to another, we ensure that the transfer is lawful and that appropriate safeguards are in place. For transfers from the European Economic Area to countries not deemed adequate by the European Commission, we rely on the most recent version of the Standard Contractual Clauses and conduct transfer impact assessments in line with regulatory guidance. For transfers involving Indian personal data, we comply with the requirements of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act and any successor legislation.

Cookies in Detail

Our cookie notice provides specific information about every cookie used on the platform, including the cookie name, purpose, expiry, and category (essential, functional, analytics, or marketing). You can adjust your cookie preferences at any time through the cookie preferences link in the footer of the platform. Disabling essential cookies will prevent the platform from functioning correctly.

Complaints to Supervisory Authorities

If you believe that our processing of your personal data infringes applicable data protection law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority. We encourage you to contact us first so we can attempt to resolve your concern directly, but you are not required to do so before approaching a regulator.

Last Updated and Version History

This Privacy Policy was last updated on the date shown at the top of this page. Prior versions are available on request. We commit to providing at least 30 days' notice in advance of any material change to this policy, except where a shorter notice period is required by law.

Additional Privacy Notes for Players

This final section collects a number of additional privacy-related notes that we believe will be useful to players who want a comprehensive understanding of how their data is handled at Raxiwin. None of the items below changes the substance of the policy above; they expand on practical aspects that often come up in player questions.

First, on shared devices: if you use Raxiwin from a device that you share with other people — a family computer, a workplace machine, or a friend's tablet — please be especially careful to log out at the end of each session and to clear browser data where appropriate. The platform does not store sensitive information in the browser beyond what is required for the session, but a logged-in session can be accessed by anyone using the same browser. For shared-device situations we recommend using a private or incognito window.

Second, on public Wi-Fi: while our connection to your device is encrypted end-to-end, public Wi-Fi networks can present other risks unrelated to our platform — for example, malicious operators of the network may attempt to direct you to phishing sites that look similar to Raxiwin. As a general rule, we recommend avoiding sensitive activity on untrusted networks, or using a reputable virtual private network if you must.

Third, on email security: many account-compromise incidents in our industry begin with the compromise of a player's email account rather than the gambling site itself. If an attacker gains control of your email, they can usually reset your gambling account password and access your account. We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication on your email account, using a unique strong password, and never reusing your email password on any other site.

Fourth, on social engineering: legitimate Raxiwin support staff will never ask you to share your password, your two-factor codes, or your private wallet keys, whether by email, SMS, phone, or in person. Anyone making such a request is impersonating us. If you ever receive such a request, please report it to security@raxiwin.com so we can investigate.

Fifth, on data minimization: we collect only the personal data we need to operate the service, comply with our legal obligations, and protect our players. We do not collect data 'just in case' it might be useful in the future. If we identify a category of data we no longer need, we delete it.

Sixth, on retention beyond the minimum: while we retain certain categories of data for the minimum periods required by law, in some cases — for example, where there is a live regulatory inquiry or a credible suspicion of fraud — retention may be extended for the duration required to resolve the matter. Extensions of this kind are documented internally and reviewed regularly.

Seventh, on derived data: in addition to the personal data we collect directly, we generate derived data through our internal systems, such as risk scores, segmentation tags, and predictive indicators of harm. Derived data of this kind is subject to the same access and security controls as the raw data from which it is derived, and is included in subject access requests where it can be meaningfully disclosed.

Eighth, on anonymization: where we use data for analytics, research, or product improvement we strive to use anonymized or aggregated data rather than personally identifying records wherever practical. Anonymized data, by definition, can no longer be linked to an identifiable individual and is not subject to data protection law in the same way as personal data.

Finally, on transparency: this policy is intended to be a complete and honest description of our data practices. If at any point you read something here that does not match your experience of the platform, please tell us — that is exactly the kind of signal we need to keep this document accurate.
