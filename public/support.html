Support Centre

Real humans, around the clock, in your language. Reach Raxiwin support through live chat, email, or our self-service Help Centre.

Here to Help, 24/7

At Raxiwin we believe great customer support is the foundation of a great gaming experience. Whether you have a question about a bonus, need help with a payment, want to update your account details, or simply want to chat about your favourite game, our support team is here for you around the clock.

Unlike many of our competitors, our support is staffed entirely with trained humans — no chatbots, no endless menus, no scripted responses. Every member of our team has gone through extensive training on every aspect of the platform and is empowered to make decisions that resolve your issue on the spot whenever possible.

How to Reach Us

We offer multiple channels for getting help, each designed for a different kind of question. Choose whichever suits you best — there's no wrong way to contact us.

Live Chat

Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including all holidays. The chat icon is visible on every page of the platform once you are logged in. Typical wait time is under 90 seconds; first-response time for technical issues averages under three minutes. Conversations are saved to your account so you can refer back to them at any time.

Email

For non-urgent questions, complex issues that require document attachments, or formal complaints, please email support@raxiwin.com. We respond to all emails within four hours during business days, and within 12 hours at all other times. Including your username and a clear description of the issue helps us help you faster.

VIP Concierge

Players in our VIP programme have access to a dedicated personal account manager available via WhatsApp, Telegram, or direct phone line. VIP support is prioritized over standard channels and includes services such as personalized bonus structuring, expedited withdrawals, and event invitations.

Help Centre

Our self-service Help Centre contains over 300 articles covering every common question, from registration walkthroughs to detailed game rules. Articles are written in plain language, illustrated with screenshots, and translated into 12 languages.

Common Issues and Quick Fixes

Before reaching out, you may be able to resolve common issues yourself in under a minute. Here are the most frequent topics:

I forgot my password

Click 'Forgot Password' on the login screen and enter your registered email. A reset link will be sent within 60 seconds; check your spam folder if you don't see it. Reset links are valid for 30 minutes.

My deposit hasn't arrived

Crypto deposits typically require a small number of network confirmations before they are credited. You can view live confirmation status in your transaction history. UPI deposits credit instantly in most cases; if you see a delay of more than five minutes, please contact support with your transaction reference.

My withdrawal is pending

Standard processing time is under five minutes for crypto and under ten minutes for UPI during business hours. If your withdrawal has been pending longer than these targets, contact support and we will investigate immediately.

I want to change my deposit limit

Limits can be reduced instantly through Account Settings → Responsible Gaming. Requests to increase a limit are subject to a 24-hour cooling-off period in line with our responsible-gaming commitments.

I want to verify my account

Visit Account Settings → Verification and upload the requested documents. Verification is typically completed within two hours during business days. You will be notified by email when verification is complete.

Filing a Complaint

If you believe something has gone wrong and you are not satisfied with the response from frontline support, you have the right to file a formal complaint. To do so, please email complaints@raxiwin.com with the subject line 'Formal Complaint' and include the following information: your username, the date and nature of the incident, a clear summary of the issue, what outcome you are seeking, and any supporting evidence such as screenshots or transaction IDs.

Formal complaints are reviewed by our complaints officer, who is independent of the operational team. We aim to acknowledge formal complaints within 24 hours and to provide a substantive response within 14 calendar days. If a complaint cannot be resolved internally, you have the right to escalate it to the relevant regulatory authority and to any alternative-dispute-resolution body listed on our website.

Account Security Issues

If you believe your account has been accessed by someone other than you, please contact us immediately through live chat or by emailing security@raxiwin.com. Our security team will lock the account pending investigation, preserve relevant logs, and help you regain control through a verified reset procedure. Where unauthorized activity has resulted in loss, we will investigate fully and, where the loss was not caused by your own breach of these Terms, we will work with you on appropriate remediation.

We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication on your account through Account Settings → Security. Two-factor authentication is the single most effective measure you can take to protect your account against credential-stuffing and phishing attacks.

Payment and Withdrawal Troubleshooting

Most payment issues fall into one of a small number of categories. A failed deposit is usually due to incorrect card details, insufficient funds, a temporary issuer block, or a country-specific restriction on gambling transactions. Re-checking the details and trying a different payment method usually resolves the issue. If not, contact your bank for an explanation of the decline before contacting us.

A withdrawal stuck in 'pending' is usually awaiting either KYC verification or a manual review of an unusually large transaction. Our team monitors the pending queue continuously and will reach out proactively if any additional information is needed. You can also message us at any time for a status update.

Bonus and Promotion Questions

If a bonus has not been credited as expected, the most common reasons are: the deposit did not meet the minimum qualifying amount; a bonus code was required but not entered; the bonus was excluded in your country; or you have already claimed the same bonus. Our support team can usually resolve missed-bonus questions on the spot once we have your transaction reference.

If you have questions about a specific bonus term — such as wagering progress, game contributions, or expiry — live chat is typically the fastest channel.

Technical and Game Issues

If a game freezes or disconnects mid-round, your stake is protected. The game state is preserved server-side, and refreshing the game should return you to where you left off. If a refresh does not work, contact support with the game name and approximate time of the issue; we can replay the round from our logs and credit any winnings due.

For broader connectivity issues — pages not loading, the cashier hanging, or persistent log-out loops — please first try clearing your browser cache or switching networks. If the issue persists, reach out to support with details of your device, browser, and approximate time the issue started, so we can investigate.

Working With Our Support Team

Our support team is here to help, and the more context you provide, the faster we can resolve your issue. Useful information typically includes your username, a clear summary of what you were trying to do, what happened instead, any error messages you saw, and the approximate time the issue occurred. Screenshots are often the single most useful thing you can share.

Please be aware that our team will never ask you for your password, your full payment-card number, or your private wallet keys. Any communication purporting to be from Raxiwin and requesting this information is fraudulent. Please report it to security@raxiwin.com.

How Our Support Team Is Structured

When you reach out to Raxiwin support, you are connecting with a globally distributed team organized around three principles: 24/7 coverage, language depth, and topic specialization. Frontline agents handle the great majority of conversations and resolve roughly nine out of ten issues without needing to escalate. When escalation is required, it goes to one of several specialist teams — payments, account verification, responsible gaming, technical, or VIP — staffed with senior specialists who can drive an issue to resolution.

Cross-team handoffs are managed through a shared case-management system so you never have to repeat yourself. The agent you speak with always has the full history of your previous conversations, the status of any pending verifications or payments, and any relevant notes from other teams.

Training and Quality Assurance

Every support agent completes a structured onboarding programme covering platform mechanics, game rules, payments, responsible gaming, regulatory requirements, and our internal escalation procedures. New agents shadow experienced colleagues for several weeks before handling conversations independently, and all conversations are subject to quality review by a dedicated quality assurance team.

We measure quality on dimensions that matter to players: was the issue resolved, was the resolution accurate, was the tone empathetic, was the response timely. We deliberately do not measure on volume-only metrics that would create incentives to rush conversations. A great support interaction takes as long as it takes to truly help the player.

Why You'll Always Speak to a Human

We have considered, and rejected, a chatbot-first support model many times over the years. The reason is simple: in a real-money environment, even a small fraction of misdirected or oversimplified responses can have serious consequences for players. A bot that confidently gives the wrong answer about a withdrawal, a bonus, or a responsible-gaming tool can do more harm than no answer at all.

We do use software internally to help agents work efficiently — surfacing relevant account information, suggesting policy references, generating routine responses for review — but every message that goes out to a player is written or approved by a real human being who has read the context.

Multilingual Support in Practice

When you contact support, our system automatically detects your account language and routes your request to an agent fluent in that language. If no native speaker is available in real time, a colleague who speaks the language well will respond as quickly as possible. We do not use machine translation for sensitive conversations such as responsible-gaming outreach or formal complaints; those are always handled by a fluent human speaker.

Information you share with support is used only to help you and is accessed only by personnel with a legitimate need. Conversations are retained in line with our Privacy Policy for quality, training, and regulatory purposes, and are subject to all of the data-protection safeguards described in that document.

Detailed Topic Guide for Support Conversations

Different topics tend to benefit from different kinds of information when you reach out. This final section is a practical guide on what to include in your message for each common topic, to help our team help you faster.

For login issues: please include the email address registered on the account, the device and browser you are using, the exact error message (a screenshot is ideal), and whether the issue occurs consistently or only intermittently. If you can no longer access the registered email address, please be ready to provide additional verification information.

For deposit issues: include the deposit amount, the payment method used, the transaction reference from the payment processor (this is critical for bank-side investigation), and the approximate time the deposit was attempted. If you have a screenshot of the failed transaction or a confirmation from the payment side, please attach it.

For withdrawal issues: include the withdrawal amount, the destination (wallet address for crypto, masked bank account for fiat), the time the withdrawal was requested, and the current status as shown in your transaction history. If you suspect the destination address may have been entered incorrectly, mention this immediately — early notice significantly improves the chances of recovery.

For bonus and promotion questions: include the bonus or promotion name (or a screenshot), the deposit that should have triggered the bonus if applicable, and a clear statement of what you expected to happen versus what actually happened.

For verification questions: include the type of document you are trying to submit, the format and size of the file, and any error message you received. If you have already submitted the document, include the approximate time of submission.

For game issues: include the game name, the game provider if known, the time the issue occurred, your bet amount, and a clear description of what you experienced. Screenshots and short screen recordings are extremely helpful for visual issues.

For account-security concerns: contact security@raxiwin.com directly rather than the general support address. Include any indicators of unauthorized access, such as unexpected login notifications, transactions you do not recognize, or changes to your account settings that you did not make. Time is often critical for security incidents.

For responsible-gaming conversations: feel free to contact responsible@raxiwin.com directly. There is no information you are required to provide; share whatever you are comfortable sharing, and our team will guide you from there. These conversations are entirely confidential.

For formal complaints: contact complaints@raxiwin.com with the subject line 'Formal Complaint' and include your username, a chronological summary of the events leading to the complaint, copies or references to any prior support conversations on the same topic, the specific outcome you are seeking, and any supporting documents. Formal complaints are reviewed by a complaints officer who is independent of frontline support.

For media and press inquiries: please direct these to press@raxiwin.com rather than general support, so we can route your request to the communications team quickly. Include your publication, your deadline, and a brief description of your story.

Above all, please remember that our team is on your side. There is no question too small, no problem too embarrassing, and no situation we have not seen before. Reach out whenever you need to — that is what we are here for.

A Final Word from the Support Team

Behind every conversation you have with Raxiwin support is a real person sitting at a desk, somewhere in the world, genuinely wanting to help you have a good day. We say this not as a marketing line but as a statement of fact about how the team is built and how the team thinks. Our agents are evaluated on whether they actually resolve issues and whether players feel heard. They are not pushed to close conversations quickly. They are not given scripts that prevent them from using judgment. They are given the context, the authority, and the training to do right by the player in front of them.

What we ask in return is patience and good faith when things go wrong. We are not a perfect company. Occasionally a deposit is delayed, a verification takes longer than it should, a bonus does not credit as expected, or a support agent gives an answer that turns out to be incomplete. When these things happen, please reach out and let us put them right. Almost every issue is easier to resolve when raised early and clearly than when it has had time to fester.

Equally, we will be honest with you when something is not what you hoped. If a withdrawal genuinely cannot be processed for a regulatory reason, we will explain why. If a bonus condition was not met, we will show you the specific term and the relevant transaction. If a complaint is not upheld, we will tell you that, and we will explain the reasoning. Honesty is a much better foundation for a long-term relationship than telling people what they want to hear.

If you take only one thing away from this page, let it be that you are welcome to reach out whenever you need anything. There is no question too small, no issue too embarrassing, no situation we have not seen before. The fastest way to a good outcome is almost always to ask.

Thank you for being a Raxiwin player. We will do our best to deserve your trust every single time you contact us.
