Welcome to the Raxiwin FAQ. We've collected the questions our support team hears most often and answered them here in plain language. If your question isn't covered below, our 24/7 live chat is just a click away.

If you are unsure, we recommend consulting a qualified legal advisor in your jurisdiction before depositing or playing.

You must be at least 18 years of age, or the legal age of majority in your jurisdiction — whichever is higher — to register and play on Raxiwin. Crypto withdrawals are typically processed within five minutes of being approved. UPI withdrawals are usually completed within ten minutes during business hours. Games & Fairness — More Detail

Some additional questions about how our games work.

How is provably fair different from RNG certification?

RNG certification means an external lab has tested the random number generator and confirmed it produces statistically random output. Provably fair takes the additional step of letting every individual player verify, after the round, that the specific result they saw was not manipulated. The two are complementary; we use both.

What is RTP and where can I find it?

RTP is the long-run average return-to-player percentage of a game. It is published in the game info panel for every slot and table game on Raxiwin. Higher is generally better, but consider volatility as well — see our blog for a deeper discussion.

Are jackpot odds disclosed?

Where the game studio publishes jackpot odds, we display them. For network jackpots shared across multiple casinos, the odds depend on the size of the shared player pool and may vary over time.

Can a game freeze cause me to lose my stake?

No. Game state is preserved server-side. A refresh will return you to your last confirmed action, and any winnings owed will be paid.
